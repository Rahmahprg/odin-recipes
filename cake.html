VANILLA CAKE RECIPE

Vanilla cake is an amazing cake enjoyed by all. It is one of the simplest cake to bake and yet most enjoyable

It is a sweet food made by bakimg a mixture of flour, eggs, sugar, and fat in an oven

Vanilla cake can be cut in large or small sizes and can be enjoyed alone or with family and friends

INGREDIENTS

For 250g of Vanilla cake, you will need

  • 250g of Flour
  • 1 tablespoon of Baking Powder
  • 225g of butter
  • 3 big eggs
  • 1 tablespoon of vanilla extract
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • Baking pan

PROCEDURES

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) grease with butter and line the baking pan with parchment paper
  2. Mix butter and sugar with a whisk till it peaks
  3. Whisk the eggs together with the vanilla extract. Pour this into the butter and sugar batter
  4. In a separate bowl, sieve the flour, baking powder together.
  5. Mix in the dry ingredients with the wet ingredient until a smooth batter in formed.
  6. Pour this batter into the pan and leave in the oven for about 15-20 minutes.
  7. Check by putting a clean toothpick into the cake to check if it is well done.
  8. It is well baked if the toothpick does not have any crumbs on it
  9. Leave to cool and enjoy with whatever beverage of your choice
